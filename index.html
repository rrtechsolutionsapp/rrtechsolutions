02A  Honest doubt

“WhatsApp se kaam chal raha hai. App kyun?”

Bilkul sahi sawaal. WhatsApp important hai, aur rahega. App WhatsApp ko replace nahi karta — app aapka permanent rate-board, catalogue aur offer counter banata hai, jo customer ke phone mein hamesha pada rehta hai.

WhatsApp tab kaam karta hai jab customer aapse baat shuru kare. App tab kaam karta hai jab customer ne abhi baat shuru bhi nahi ki.

Jewellery mein sale aksar “aaj rate kya hai?” ya “koi design dekha tha” se shuru hoti hai. App wahi first thought aapki shop ke naam se connect karta hai.

Only WhatsApp

  • Customer ko pehle message karna padta hai.
  • Rate aur offer chat mein neeche dab jaate hain.
  • New designs sirf selected logon ko forward hote hain.
  • Staff busy ho toh reply late hota hai.

App + WhatsApp

  • Customer khud rate check kar leta hai.
  • Offer, catalogue aur shop identity ek jagah rehti hai.
  • Push notification se festival ya new arrival seedha dikhta hai.
  • Interested customer app se call/WhatsApp par aa jaata hai.
